## 1. Wedding E-Invitation — Standard (Protocol Archive)

**Idea**
A multi-section editorial wedding e-invitation for Wei Jun & Mei Ling, getting married on 28 December 2026 at The Majestic Hotel, Kuala Lumpur. Features GSAP scroll animations, an editorial photo gallery, and a WhatsApp RSVP call-to-action.

**Theme**
Editorial minimal — the "Protocol Archive" direction. Warm ivory (#FFF9F5) background with stone (#78716C) text and champagne gold (#C9A84C) accents. Gradient-border section dividers with glass-morphism detail cards. Elegant serif heading font paired with a light sans-serif body. GSAP-powered text reveal, image settle, and staggered entrance animations on scroll.

**Content**
Hero section: couple's names "Wei Jun & Mei Ling" in large editorial serif font, wedding date "28.12.2026", and a full-bleed background photo with soft overlay. Event details section: two glass-morphism cards — ceremony (3:00 PM, Garden Pavilion) and dinner reception (7:00 PM, Grand Ballroom) — each with venue name, time, and dress code "Semi-Formal". Venue section: "The Majestic Hotel, Kuala Lumpur" with a tap-to-navigate Google Maps link. Story gallery section: 4 couple photos in a styled editorial grid with grayscale-to-color hover effect. RSVP section: heading "Will You Join Us?" with a prominent WhatsApp CTA button "RSVP via WhatsApp" (no embedded form). Footer with full Open Graph meta preview hint and "Share this invitation" link. Mobile-responsive layout optimized for phone viewing.
