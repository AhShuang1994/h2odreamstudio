## Copy Notes

- **Tone calibration:** Warm and direct, avoids corporate jargon. Uses "you/your" to speak directly to the reader.
- **CN register:** Simplified Chinese with Malaysian Chinese spoken tone (e.g., "搞定", "上手", "好看"). Avoids overly formal Mainland phrasing.
- **CTA strategy:** Each button uses action verb + possessive ("My") to create ownership feeling. WhatsApp messages include a bracket placeholder so the user fills in their specific detail — this increases reply quality.
- **Techniques applied:** Concrete benefit headlines (not feature lists), specific delivery timeframes for trust, and pain-point-aware taglines (Card 3: visitors -> customers, Card 4: don't lose customers).
