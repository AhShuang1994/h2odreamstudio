The Art of the Wok

At Wok & Flame, we ignite the senses. We are driven by a passion for the primal element of fire, transforming traditional Malaysian classics into avant-garde culinary experiences. We source only the most premium ingredients, letting the intense heat of the wok and the smoky whisper of the grill elevate every dish to cinematic intensity. Welcome to a moody, sensory-rich dining room where every bite tells a story of heritage and innovation.
